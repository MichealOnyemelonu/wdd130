Nigeria

Nigeria, also known as the Federal Republic of Nigeria is a country in West Africa on the Gulf of Guinea, the most populous on the African continent. The country's name comes from the Niger River.

It first appeared in the British newspaper The Times in 1897. The country borders Benin, Niger, Chad, and Cameroon. It is currently a member of the African Union and ECOWAS.
